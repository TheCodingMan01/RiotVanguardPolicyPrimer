About this project

This website was created by Chris Daniells as part of the Online Power and Resistance unit Policy Primer assignment at Curtin University.

It examines the privacy and consent implications of Riot Games’ kernel-level anti-cheat software, Riot Vanguard.

No AI-generated images or text are included in the public submission.
