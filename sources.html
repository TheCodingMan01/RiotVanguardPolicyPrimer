Want to dig deeper?

If you want more on kernel level anti-cheat, consent, and privacy in games, start here. I play too, so this is the mix I found useful: quick reads, official info, news, and broader research that informed the main primer.

IMPORTANT!! Quick tips for players

  • Check three things first: what data is collected, how long it’s stored, and how you can appeal.
  • Use the right PC: play on a device you’re comfortable giving kernel-level access to.
  • Compare sources: read Riot’s privacy notice, then check what independent outlets report.
